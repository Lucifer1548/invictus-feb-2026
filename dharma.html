Ethical Interpretation

Your dilemma reflects the eternal tension between Artha (material prosperity) and Dharma (righteous duty). The Gita teaches that true success lies not in external achievements but in performing one's duty with detachment and integrity. Your current path may offer immediate gains but could lead to long-term spiritual dissonance.

Practical Advice

  • Consider alternative paths that honor both your career goals and ethical principles

  • Seek guidance from mentors who embody both professional success and ethical conduct
  • Remember that sustainable success comes from alignment, not compromise
